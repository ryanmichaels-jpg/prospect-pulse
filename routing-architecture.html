Routing pass — turning counter-bets into operational instructions

Today, a counter-bet (JVM disqualifier, planned pure-infra shop, planned sub-ten team) subtracts points from a company's score and stops. The score is the SDR's prioritization input — but it doesn't say how to approach a flagged account. The proposed routing pass converts each counter-bet outcome into a named outbound motion: which seats to target, which to avoid, what to lead with.
